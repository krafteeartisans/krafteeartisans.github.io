About

What you need to know about Kraftee

Artisan designers generate most of their income from international tourists. Tourists spend significant amounts of money on souvenirs and craft products and are willing to pay more for artisan works than local buyers. With the COVID-19 pandemic and the subsequent travel bans, artisan designers face a significant drop in their sales or are even forced to stop producing. In the meantime, the situation has caused a significant increase in online sales. We Kraftee, are trying to get artisans and product lovers back on stage by providing a platform that serves as an intermediary between both. From now, an artisan does not need the physical presence of a customer to be able to sell.
